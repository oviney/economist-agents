    Risk Mitigation¶

    Quality Preservation: - All quality gates preserved (DoR, DoD, Visual QA) - SM Agent enforces gates, cannot bypass - Human PO validates deliverables (final safety net)

    Agent Hallucination: - Temperature=0 for deterministic decisions - Self-validation before signaling completion - Human reviews all decisions if error rate >15%
